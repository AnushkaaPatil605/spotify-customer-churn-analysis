# 🎵 Spotify Customer Churn Analysis

> An end-to-end Data Analytics project focused on identifying customer churn drivers, analyzing user behavior, and providing actionable business recommendations using **Python** and **Power BI**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the biggest challenges for subscription-based platforms like Spotify. This project analyzes customer engagement, listening behavior, subscription plans, and support interactions to identify the key factors contributing to customer churn.

Using Python for data cleaning and exploratory analysis, followed by an interactive Power BI dashboard, this project transforms raw data into meaningful business insights and strategic recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Identify the major factors influencing customer churn.
- Analyze customer engagement and listening behavior.
- Compare churn across subscription plans.
- Identify high-risk and loyal customer segments.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
- Provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Premium subscribers exhibit significantly lower churn than Free users.
- Low customer engagement is the strongest predictor of churn.
- Users inactive for longer periods are more likely to leave the platform.
- Higher support ticket counts are associated with increased churn.
- Customers who create more playlists demonstrate stronger long-term retention.

---

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Improve personalized music recommendations for low-engagement users.
- Launch targeted campaigns to re-engage inactive customers.
- Encourage playlist creation through personalized and collaborative playlists.
- Offer promotional Premium plans to high-risk Free users.
- Improve customer support response time and issue resolution.
- Monitor customer engagement continuously to identify churn risk early.
